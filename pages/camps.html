Our Innovation Camps

On this page we will post our upcoming Innovation Camps. A rule of thumb is that we will host an "afternoon camp" the first wednesday of each month at our offices. Besides that we will try to attend bigger conferences and make our presence known at other relevant venues. And as always, if you want us to customize a training/study camp for your businees or school - please do not hesitate to Contact Us.

When?

We host Innovation Camps every month, at a minimum. We also set up camps after demand.

Where?

We have montly camps in-house, at our offices. But we try to move around as much as possible, so if you have any suggestions or requests. Please do not hesitate to Contact Us.

How much?

We have a very flexible pricing structure, and we guarantee that our camps will cost below 50€ per day per participant. With volume discounts for bigger groups, and special discounts will be posted regularly.
